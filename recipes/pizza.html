Pizza

Description:

Best pizza ever, made with love!

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Broccoli
  • Onions
  • Olives

Steps:

  • Roll out dough
  • Spoon sauce and spread
  • Sprinkle cheese all over
  • Arrange toppings
  • Bake at 450 for 15 minutes
