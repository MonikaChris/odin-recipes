Salad

Description:

Who needs a recipe for a salad you might ask? Why you do my friend! This is the best salad ever!

Ingredient:

  • Romaine Lettuce (greenest you can find)
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Banana Peppers
  • Olives
  • Scallions
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Ranch Dressing

Steps:

  • Chop all ingredients, add to bowl, top with dressing.
  • Enjoy!
